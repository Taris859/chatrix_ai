What is Roleplay AI Chat?

Roleplay AI chat is a collaborative writing format where a user and an artificial intelligence model co-create an interactive text adventure. The user writes their character's actions and speech, and the AI acts as both the supporting cast and the surrounding world, responding in character.

By leveraging customized system prompts and scene contexts, you instruct the AI model to follow specific rules. For example, you can tell the model to play a medieval knight in a fantasy realm, a rogue netrunner in a cyberpunk city, or even act as a Game Master (GM) directing a tabletop-style adventure. The AI adjusts its vocabulary, tone, and decision-making to fit the narrative rules of the chosen universe.

The Mechanics of Interactive Text Roleplay

To create an immersive story, writers follow standard formatting rules that distinguish actions from spoken dialogue:

  • Actions and Expressions: Actions, body language, and environmental descriptions are typically wrapped in asterisks (e.g., *slides the key across the table, watching your reaction closely*). This tells the AI what is happening in the physical space of the scene.
  • Dialogue: Spoken lines are written in standard quotation marks (e.g., "We need to leave before the guards return.").
  • Setting the Scene: By adding a "scene context" inside Chatrix, you supply the AI with a background description of the setting (e.g., "A dimly lit tavern during a thunderstorm"). The AI uses this context to color its descriptions and actions.
